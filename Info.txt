Classical vs Quantum — Why Grover's is revolutionary (√N speedup)

Iteration Effects — Show how amplitude grows with each Oracle+Diffusion cycle
3-Qubit Grover — Finding |101⟩ among 8 states (more realistic)

Multiple Targets — Finding 2 marked states simultaneously

Key Components:

Oracle: Marks target with phase flip (-1)
Diffusion: "Inversion about average" — amplifies marked state
Repeat: ~√N times for optimal results

The Magic:
Classical: 4 items → 2 guesses on average
Quantum:   4 items → 1 Grover iteration (90%+ success)
For N=1,000,000: Classical needs ~500k tries, Grover needs ~1000 tries. 500x speedup!
